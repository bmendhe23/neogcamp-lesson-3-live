this is my coding story

Let me be frank I did not knew something like this even existed because I was born and brought up in a defense family and the only career I ever thought of was being a Army Officer.

This is until I passed my 10th standard and got to know about IIT-JEE. This is where it all started and talking about the start, it was the worst start in programming I could have xD. While the start was worst, I managed to make the ending of my boards somewhat good.

here I am a under-grad now

This is the part where I learn most of the programming and I start enjoying programming.

"Do not fear failure but rather fear not trying.” ― Roy T. Bennett

I did something in a similar way. My failures consisted of not understanding what was going on to what should I do next. It was not very hard to get overwhelmed my everything in the starting. But, as they say practice makes you perfect, I simply followed my course and saw improvement in my problem solving. This pushed me to learn more.

I somehow managed to learn things and also taught my juniors the same when they came to me. It is a wholesome experience to learn new things. It has been some time since I have coded as I was preparing for my MCA entrance.

the journey starts again...

Now, I am a student of NIT, Tiruchirappalli and I am at this very moment when I am writing this blog and learning web development from Tanay sir and it has been very interesting so far.

The journey of my coding has started again with my masters and I plan to learn a lot more great things in the coming future.

“Curiosity about life in all of its aspects, I think, is still the secret of great creative people.” – Leo Burnett

Hope you all liked the blog. Please let me know if I should write more of them and keep on documenting my journey as I learn more things.
